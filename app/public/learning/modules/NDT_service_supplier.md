# 서비스 공급자 관리 (Service Supplier)

**Service Supplier Management**

- Project: `PROJ-2026-HC-001`
- Category: `service_supplier`
- Extracted clauses: **1**

## 개요 (Overview)

**KO:** NDT 용역을 수행하는 firm(서비스 공급자)의 승인, 자격, 감사 등 관리 체계에 관한 LR 요건.

**EN:** LR requirements for approval, qualification, and audit of firms (service suppliers) providing NDT services.

## 추출된 조항 (Extracted Clauses)

### 1. `ndt_service_supplier_01`
- Methods: **-**

> Firms engaged in non-destructive testing services shall be approved as service
suppliers by LR, subject to periodic audit.

- **UT** Ultrasonic · **RT** Radiographic · **MT** Magnetic Particle
- **PT** Liquid Penetrant · **VT** Visual · **ET** Eddy Current
