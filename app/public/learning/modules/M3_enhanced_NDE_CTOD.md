**EN:** Enhanced NDE with stricter acceptance criteria per ShipRight. CTOD ≥ 0.18 mm at minimum design temperature. EGW not permitted.

## 추출된 NDT 조항 (Extracted NDT Clauses)

### `measure_3_ctod`
- Scope: CTOD at minimum design temperature
- Rule ref: `Pt 4, Ch 8, 2.3.10(d)`

> Where enhanced NDE is adopted as Measure 3, CTOD value shall not be less than 0.18 mm at the minimum design temperature.

## CTOD 요건 (CTOD Requirement)

> CTOD value shall not be less than **0.18 mm** at the minimum design temperature.

**Note:** CTOD is part of Measure 3 enhanced NDE, **not** Measure 2.
